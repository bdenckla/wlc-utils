In the LC it is often difficult to tell whether a mark is a merkha, a tipexa, or meteg because merkha and tipexa often lack an inclination that would distinguish them from meteg. Yet, from context, we can usually determine the most likely intended meaning, and that is the case here with שרי. To transcribe the mark on שרי as tipexa is an aggressively uncharitable transcription: tipexa is the least likely of the three possible meanings of this mark. Merkha is by far the most likely.
